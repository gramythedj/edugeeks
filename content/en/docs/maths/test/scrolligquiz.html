From shopping to booking travel, chatting with friends to paying bills online, we rely on online companies for a variety of services. Cybercrime is a serious issue, and it's important that we take steps to protect ourselves. To solve this issue, several governments of the world are taking good measures. But there is another issue that often goes unnoticed: online companies taking advantage of people. Many legitimate companies engage in questionable practices that deceive consumers.
